The mission of NextLevel Redmond is to provide education in unique skills critical to success in high school and beyond. Our course selection is based on our own skills and experience, as well as our goal to build future leaders with expertise in lifestyle, career, and academia. Our current courses, Public Speaking and World Culinary Arts, are tailored towards skills important in students' lives beyond middle school and high school. Our course selection is set to expand to include Introduction to Business and Personal Finance.

  • PUBLIC SPEAKING AND PROFESSIONAL COMMUNICATION

    Jay Dharmadhikari is a two-time State Competitor in Congressional Debate and the Vice President of the Redmond High School Speech and Debate club. He has partnered with NextLevel Education of Redmond to offer FREE online speech & debate classes starting in June to take your child's speech & debate skills to the high-school level. This online class is for any middle-schooler looking to learn about high-school pathways for public speaking and skills for professional communication used in the real world. Over the course of 4 weeks, students will learn types of public speaking, how to prepare for presentations and interviews, and tips for effective speaking and delivery.
